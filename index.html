About

02 / Profile

I'm Eldee Benjamin — a multidisciplinary designer with a passion for storytelling through design. With a strong foundation in typography, image editing, and layout, I specialize in crafting intentional visual experiences that bridge print, digital, and 3D mockup environments.

From brand identity systems to promotional campaigns and packaging, I bring a keen eye for detail and a strategic mindset to every project — whether it's elevating a product on a shelf or building engagement through social media.

I thrive in collaborative settings, using creativity, adaptability, and communication to bring bold concepts to life and drive results.
